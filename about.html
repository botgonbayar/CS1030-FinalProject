Why I Created Cybersecurity Quick Check

Connect with me on LinkedIn

I built this site to help everyday internet users learn about cybersecurity in a fun and approachable way. A lot of people think online safety is only for tech experts, but even simple steps can make a huge difference. Whether it's recognizing a phishing email or using better passwords, these habits can protect you from real threats.

This project was built as part of an introductory web development course. It includes a quiz to test your cybersecurity knowledge, helpful tips and resources, and a focus on accessibility and responsive design. Everything was created using HTML, CSS, and a little JavaScript.

My hope is that this website makes cybersecurity feel less intimidating and encourages people to build better digital habits.
