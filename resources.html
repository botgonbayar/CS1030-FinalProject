Basic Cybersecurity Tips

Simple ways to protect yourself online

Use strong passwords: Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password across sites.

Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA): Add an extra layer of protection to your accounts.

Think before you click: Hover over links and verify the source before opening emails or attachments.

Keep software updated: Regular updates patch known vulnerabilities.

Use secure Wi-Fi networks: Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks. Use a VPN when possible.
